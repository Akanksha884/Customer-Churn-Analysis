# Business Insights

## Insight 1: Month-to-Month Contracts Have the Highest Churn

**Observation:**
Customers with month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rate compared to one-year and two-year contracts.

**Recommendation:**
Encourage customers to switch to long-term contracts by offering discounts, loyalty rewards, or exclusive benefits.

---

## Insight 2: Fiber Optic Customers Are More Likely to Churn

**Observation:**
Customers using Fiber Optic internet service have a higher churn rate than DSL and No Internet Service customers.

**Recommendation:**
Improve service quality, resolve network issues quickly, and provide better customer support for Fiber Optic users.

---

## Insight 3: Electronic Check Users Show High Churn

**Observation:**
Customers paying through Electronic Check have the highest churn among all payment methods.

**Recommendation:**
Promote automatic payment methods such as Credit Card or Bank Transfer by providing cashback or discounts.

---

## Insight 4: High Monthly Charges Increase Churn

**Observation:**
Customers with higher monthly charges are more likely to leave the company.

**Recommendation:**
Introduce affordable plans, bundled offers, and personalized discounts for high-paying customers.

---

## Insight 5: Short-Tenure Customers Leave More Frequently

**Observation:**
Customers with low tenure have a significantly higher probability of churn.

**Recommendation:**
Improve onboarding experience and engage new customers through welcome offers and early support.

---

## Insight 6: Customers Without Tech Support Churn More

**Observation:**
Customers who do not subscribe to Tech Support services have a higher churn rate.

**Recommendation:**
Offer free trials or discounted Tech Support packages to increase customer retention.

---

## Insight 7: Customers Without Online Security Are at Higher Risk

**Observation:**
Customers without Online Security services are more likely to churn.

**Recommendation:**
Bundle Online Security with internet plans to increase perceived value.

---

## Insight 8: Senior Citizens Need Better Retention Strategies

**Observation:**
Senior citizen customers exhibit relatively higher churn compared to younger customers.

**Recommendation:**
Provide dedicated customer care, simplified plans, and personalized assistance for senior citizens.

---

## Insight 9: Long-Term Customers Generate More Revenue

**Observation:**
Customers with longer tenure contribute significantly higher total revenue and show lower churn.

**Recommendation:**
Reward loyal customers with exclusive benefits, discounts, and loyalty programs to maintain long-term relationships.

---

## Insight 10: Focus on High-Risk Customer Segments

**Observation:**
The highest-risk customers are those with Month-to-Month contracts, Fiber Optic internet, Electronic Check payment method, and high monthly charges.

**Recommendation:**
Develop targeted retention campaigns for these customer segments to reduce churn and improve customer lifetime value.
